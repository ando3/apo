Who are we?

Rush Video: Watch Here

Alpha Phi Omega is the nation's largest co-educational community service fraternity. Established in 1925 and has grown to include chapters across the United States, Canada, the Philippines, and Australia. Rho Pi is Alpha Phi Omega's chapter in University of California, San Diego and is the largest professional fraternity on campus.

Every chapter revolves around three cardinal principles: Leadership, Friendship and Service. We aim to develop our understanding of these three principles and strive to provide service to the fraternity, to the campus, to the community and to the country.

We are all-inclusive and invite you to explore the opportunities that Alpha Phi Omega can provide!

Our Three Cardinal Principles

the foundation of the fraternity

Leadership

As a professional fraternity, we seek to develop leaders by offering opportunities to get involved! We have a multitude of leadership positions for both pledges and actives. These opportunities include large-scale event planning to reaching out to other chapters at the national level. With a supportive community, you won't be alone in your path to becoming a leader.

Friendship

Brotherhood is the spirit of friendship. Our fraternity is built on the foundation of brotherhood, and we emphasize that through activities and fellowships for members to get together. Sports, competition, food adventures, dances and travel awaits - we welcome everyone to join and find lifelong friends!

Service

Every year, Alpha Phi Omega Rho Pi completes over 7,000 hours of community service. We aim to serve our chapter, our campus, our community and our country. Our service program is extremely diverse, with many types of community service. We are always open to new projects while continuing our traditional quarterly service projects.

FAQ

frequently asked questions

Who is allowed to join?
We are a co-educational fraternity and allow all genders to join. We are also open to any student in good academic standing. As a professional fraternity, we do not conflict with your ability to join other social fraternities or organizations.

Does Alpha Phi Omega haze?
No. The University of California, San Diego along with the national organization of Alpha Phi Omega and our chapter of Alpha Phi Omega: Rho Pi all have expressive policies against any form of hazing. We do not condone any form of degredation, personal service and other practices that violate the principles of Alpha Phi Omega and The University of California, San Diego.

If I decide to rush, am I committed to joining Alpha Phi Omega?
No. The rush process provides an opportunity for you to evaluate Alpha Phi Omega and see if it is right for you. During rush, if you decide for any reason not to move forward in Alpha Phi Omega, you are in no way obligated.

How is APO different from a social Greek fraternity or sorority?
APO is a fraternity in that we are a Greek letter organization, yet we are not a social fraternity. We are a national organization with rituals, traditions, and a unique bond among our many members. Community Service is always our primary focus. We are not associated with the Inter-fraternity Council (IFC), the Panhellenic Association nor the Multicultural Greek Council (MGC). We are instead the premier National Co-Educational Community Service Fraternity in the Nation. At our core is the belief that everybody can be great because everybody can serve and do good in the community.
